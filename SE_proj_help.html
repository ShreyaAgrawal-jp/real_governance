LET KASHMIR SPEAK

The population of Kashmir is demanding the right for self-determination as mandated by the United Nations resolutions. They have the right to object to what appears to be a dictatorship as opposed to a democracy at present.
JUSTICE FOR HATHRAS VICTIM

If you really care about your daughters and women of this country- arise, wake up, speak up, and demand justice.
STOP TARGATE ABUSE AND HARASSMENT OF WOMEN ON SOCIAL MEDIA

The time is now to choose a side! We can choose the path of safety or be mere spectators to the abuse our women face online.


Uphold Right to Live with Dignity for Women in Marital Discord

In spite of various existing laws and judgement delivered by the Supreme court of India, the fact remains that women are not getting maintenance.
Increase health budget to 10% of GDP

India has one of the least health spends in the world - a dismal 1.3%. Advanced nations spend almost 14-18% of their GDP on health, and even poor countries like Bangladesh and Sri Lanka spend more on the health of their citizens than India does.
Political Representation for the Transgender Community

Ask for adequate political representation of the ‘Transgender Community’ so that we move towards a truly inclusive India.


Declare Domestic Violence Prevention Service as Essential Services

Ask the Ministry of Women and Child Development to put in place Standard Operating Procedures for emergency situations at the national level so that victims of violence are not left adrift.

#DignityInFloods

We may not be able to stop the floods from striking Assam, but the least we can do is ensure dignity during disaster for all.
Introduce 'Mental Wellness Hour' in Schools

Schools play a very critical part in the foundation of a young person both physically and mentally. While we take measures to watch over the physical health of a youngster, are we doing enough to say the same about their mental health?

Broadcast Child Helpline On All TV Channels

Most of the children inside their homes do not even know how to ask for help. They are not even aware that the child helpline number is 1098, leave alone how to protect themselves!
